## What pBTC is

pBTC is a free, **open-source (GPL-3.0)** public good: a trust-minimized Bitcoin →
PulseChain bridge. It is experimental, testnet / pre-production, and unaudited.

## No token, no sale

- **pBTC will not issue a token and will not run a token sale.**
- **There is no airdrop**, presale, or future token — do not donate expecting one.
- **pBTC** the asset is a **1:1 Bitcoin-backed utility token** (a bridge receipt),
  not an investment product, and it carries no yield for holding it.

## Donations are gifts

By donating you acknowledge and agree that:

1. **You receive nothing in return** — no token, equity, shares, revenue,
   governance rights, profit share, goods, or services.
2. **No expectation of profit.** A donation is not an investment. The project
   makes no representation about any future value and you should expect **no
   financial return**.
3. **Use of funds.** Donations are used at the maintainers' discretion to
   develop, audit, operate, and promote the open-source project.
4. **No refunds.** On-chain donations are irreversible.
5. **Not a securities offering.** A donation is a voluntary gift to a public good,
   not the purchase of a security or any other financial instrument.
6. **Taxes are your responsibility.** The project gives no tax advice.
7. **Eligibility.** You represent that you are not a sanctioned person or in a
   sanctioned jurisdiction, and that donating is lawful for you.
8. **No warranty.** The software is provided under GPL-3.0 **as is**, without
   warranty of any kind.

## Incentives are compensation for services, not returns

Where the project rewards contributors or validators/guardians, those rewards are
**fee-share compensation for work performed** (liveness, watchtowering, relay
maintenance), paid in existing assets (e.g. pBTC or PLS) — **never a newly issued
token**, and never a passive return on a contribution.

## Transparency

When a treasury is established: the official donation address(es) will be
published in the project's official channels (repository + verified socials) so a
cloned-site scam cannot substitute its own, funds will be held in a **multi-
signature treasury** (no single person/host/cloud), and use of funds will be
reported publicly.
